Memogotchi is a wellness app that turns your digital habits into self-awareness.  It combines three ideas into one: a virtual pet (Memogotchi) that reflects your screen health, an analog twin that learns your interests and nudges you toward offline alternatives, and a diary with time-capsule entries you write to your future self.


Unlike the popular tamagotchi / virtual pets that thrives on attention, Memo weakens when the user doom-scrolls and recovers health when the user stops doing so. The pet observes your usage patterns, then suggests alternative real-world activities. Overtime, Memo builds a personality profile based on the user’s activity. It specially grows offline. The battery system lets the user log their emotional, social, physical, and motivation levels, turning your feelings to something that is visible and actionable. The diary lets you lock entries behind conditions, for you to open when you’re sad, on certain dates (birthday, anniversary, etc.), if heartbroken, etc.
